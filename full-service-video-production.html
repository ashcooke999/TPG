How It Works?

Let The Podcast Guys take your content further!

1

Plan

Success begins with meticulous planning and an understanding of what good looks like to you and your brand. This is where the video expertise of The Podcast Guys comes into play, identifying how we can best place your brand on screen either to complement your podcast or as a stand-alone production.


Video allows us to further elevate your brand's visibility and engagement. Through dynamic collaboration and in-depth ideation sessions, we devise a bespoke and creative video production strategy.

2

Create

Our dedicated, in-house production team facilitates both on-location and studio recordings, for video podcasts, promotional content, live-stream, webinars and fireside chats. This gives you flexible solutions to meet your business objectives. Utilising our state-of-the-art video production equipment, we unlock the narrative that echoes your brand’s core values.


Our specialist Visual Designers and Video Editors refine your content by gathering and recording interviews, relevant Broll footage and pairing this with captivating sound design to align with your crafted identity, encapsulating professionalism and credibility. Through a structured 360-degree stakeholder feedback loop, we ensure alignment with your strategic goals, accommodating valuable insights and feedback at every stage.

3

Launch

We devise a highly targeted promotional strategy, from bespoke social media video content to a fully branded campaign, that’s designed to drive your audiences to the places you want them to go.


Whether it’s effective PR & Media outreach initiatives or expert management of your dedicated YouTube channel, we position your brand prominently and with purpose across your chosen internal and external platforms.
