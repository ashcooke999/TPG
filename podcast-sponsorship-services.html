The Numbers Speak For Themselves...

More receptive to podcast ads

Audiences are 3x more receptive to podcast ads than ads shown on TV. In fact, podcasts are found to be a more trusted source than any other media channel with 48% of UK listeners saying that they trust podcast hosts more than any other host.

2.5bn

Projected podcast ad spend by 2024

In 2022, the UK enjoyed it’s biggest ever advertising expenditure, hitting £80 million. In the US, this was substantially higher at $1.73billion. The US podcast advertising expenditure is expected to double in 2024.

Brand safety rating

Compared to visual and social platforms, the use of podcast advertising is perceived as the most ‘brand safe’ at 83% amongst audiences, particularly due to podcasts having the ability to align with listener interests.
